Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Insyra ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Insyra mobile application.

Information We Collect

Insyra does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app allows users to enter mood reflections and emotional notes. This content is stored locally on your device.

AI Processing

To provide emotional insights and pattern analysis, user-entered text may be securely sent to a third-party AI service (OpenAI) for processing. This data is used only to generate insights within the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your mood entries and reflections are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a backend database of user accounts or personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data sent to the AI provider is processed solely for generating insights and is not used to identify you.

Analytics and Tracking

Insyra does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

Insyra is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
