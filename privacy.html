Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Insyra ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Insyra mobile application.

Information We Collect

Insyra does not require account registration and does not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app allows users to create mood reflections, emotional notes, and related journaling content. This content is stored locally on your device.

AI Processing & Data Transmission

When AI features are used (for example: generating insights, reflections, or goals), the app may securely transmit certain user-entered content to our third-party AI processing provider: OpenAI.

Data that may be sent to OpenAI includes:

  • Journal text you type into the app
  • Selected mood (and optional emotional metadata such as tone/energy if you provide it)
  • Basic request context needed to generate the AI response inside the app

This data is sent only to generate the requested AI output inside Insyra. We do not use this data for advertising, tracking, profiling, or marketing.

Data Storage

Your mood entries and reflections are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a backend database of user accounts or store your journal content on our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal information. We share data only with OpenAI when you choose to use AI features, and only for the purpose of generating the AI response within the app.

Analytics and Tracking

Insyra does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

Insyra is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
